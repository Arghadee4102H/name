What Is Identity Forge?

Identity Forge Generator creates randomized, realistic identity profiles for testing, prototyping, UX design, and data population. All identities are entirely fictional and randomly generated — no real personal data is used or stored.

Each profile includes country-appropriate names, valid date of birth (18+), and realistic email formats. Results can be copied individually or downloaded as a text file.
